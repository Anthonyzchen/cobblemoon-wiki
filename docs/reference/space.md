## Ad Astra — Getting to Space

- Build a rocket, then **place it on a Launch Pad** — it won't launch sitting on the ground.  
- **Fuel**:  
	- Pump **oil** (Oil Wells spawn in oceans) then refine oil → fuel in a **Fuel Refinery** and fill the rocket.  
	- **Cryo Fuel** (efficient alternative — a full tank goes further) — freeze **ice / blue ice / packed ice** in a **Cryo Freezer**.  
- Most planets have **no air** — wear a full **Space Suit and come with extra oxygen or be prepared to setup a base** or you'll suffocate.

**The planets in order of difficulty to reach:**  
- Moon - Tier 1 Rocket  
- Mars - Tier 2 Rocket  
- Venus / Verdara - Tier 3 Rocket  
- Glacio - Tier 4 Rocket

Each planet has themed Pokémon.

**Verdara** is a warm jungle/desert planet built from existing Overworld biomes — jungle, desert, savanna, badlands, dark forest, mangrove, volcanic, plus lush / dripstone / deep-dark caves. Cobblemon spawns by biome, so **any Pokémon whose dex entry lists one of those biomes (or "any Overworld biome") also spawns on Verdara** (~684 species). It's the off-world spot to farm warm-biome Pokémon — and the legendary keys gated to those biomes.
