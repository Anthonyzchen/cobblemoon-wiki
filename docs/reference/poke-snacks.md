## Poké Snacks (Spawn Manipulation)

Cook a **Poké Snack** in a **Campfire Pot**, then place the block — it spawns Pokémon nearby until it's eaten away. The pot has **3 seasoning slots**, so one snack can carry **up to 3 stacked effects**.

The same seasonings also work on **Poké Bait** for fishing. A few effects only apply to one or the other (noted below).

**Type Boost** — ×10 spawn weight for that type:  
Chilan → Normal  
Tanga → Bug  
Colbur → Dark  
Haban → Dragon  
Wacan → Electric  
Roseli → Fairy  
Chople → Fighting  
Occa → Fire  
Coba → Flying  
Kasib → Ghost  
Rindo → Grass  
Shuca → Ground  
Yache → Ice  
Kebia → Poison  
Payapa → Psychic  
Charti → Rock  
Babiri → Steel  
Passho → Water

**Egg Group Boost** — ×10 spawn weight for those groups:  
Rawst → Field  
Pecha → Water 3, Bug  
Cheri → Grass, Fairy  
Chesto → Human-Like, Flying  
Aspear → Water 1, Water 2  
Persim → Mineral, Amorphous  
Lum → Dragon, Monster

**Gender** — 25% chance to force the spawn's gender:  
Kee → Female  
Maranga → Male

*No effect on genderless Pokémon (Ditto, Magnemite line, most legendaries) or single-gender species.*

**Nature** — chance to roll a nature that raises that stat:  
Attack: Razz 25% · Figy 50% · Touga 75% · Spelon 100%  
Defense: Pinap 25% · Iapapa 50% · Nomel 75% · Belue 100%  
Sp. Atk: Bluk 25% · Wiki 50% · Cornn 75% · Pamtre 100%  
Sp. Def: Wepear 25% · Aguav 50% · Rabuta 75% · Durin 100%  
Speed: Nanab 25% · Mago 50% · Magost 75% · Watmel 100%

**IVs** — +5 IVs in one stat:  
Lansat HP · Liechi Atk · Ganlon Def · Petaya SpA · Apicot SpD · Salac Spe

**EVs** — +100 EVs in one stat:  
Pomeg HP · Kelpsy Atk · Qualot Def · Hondew SpA · Grepa SpD · Tamato Spe

**Shiny** — extra rolls against the shiny rate (1/8192 here):  
Golden Apple ×1 (≈2× odds)  
Starf Berry ×4 (≈5× odds)  
Enchanted Golden Apple ×9 (≈10× odds)

**Spawn Speed** — cuts the delay between spawns:  
Sitrus / Apple −50%  
Oran −33%  
Glow Berries / Custap / Micle / Golden Apple −25%  
Sweet Berries −12.5%  
Enchanted Golden Apple −10%

**Rarity Boost** — pushes spawns toward rarer buckets:  
Golden Carrot / Glistering Melon Slice / Golden Apple +1  
Enchanted Golden Apple +10

**Other:**  
Enigma → 5% chance of a Hidden Ability  
Hopo → +10 levels · Leppa → +5 levels  
Jaboca → +100 friendship  
Rowap → rerolls the Pokémon's drops

**Stacking:** ingredients of the same effect **add together**, and duplicates count. Two Enchanted Golden Apples = 18 shiny rolls; three Chilan = ×30 Normal. Chance-based effects (gender, Hidden Ability) also add, but cap at 100%.

**Fishing only:** Custap (70%) and Micle (100%) raise the chance a Pokémon bites instead of junk. This does nothing on a placed snack.

**Note:** **Chilan** is the only berry that boosts **Ditto** — Ditto is Normal-type, and its egg group has no berry.
