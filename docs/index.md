**Pokédex Wiki — how to get every Pokémon**

This is the full National Dex (#1–1025) for the pack. We moved a lot of spawns around and legendaries are gated behind key items, so the existing information only gets you part of the way. Posts below run **#1 → #1025 in order** — each entry lists its biome(s), and how to obtain or evolve it. When a Pokémon spawns in more than one dimension, biomes are listed per dimension.

**Live lookup while playing:** the **PokeNav** (in-game item) shows the spawn pool for the area you're standing in. [Cobblemon](https://wiki.cobblemon.com/index.php/Main_Page) has extra details.

**Legendaries:** most don't spawn naturally — get the **Key item** (quest, chest, or trade for it), hold it, and **right-click in the correct biome** → it spawns and the key is consumed.

**Exceptions that DO spawn wild** (rare/ultra-rare, in themed biomes — see their dex posts): Some **legendaries**, the **Paradox** Pokémon and **Ultra Beasts**.

**Spawns are heavily redistributed** — many Pokémon live in different biomes/dimensions than base Cobblemon, and each planet has themed spawns. Use this wiki or the in-game **PokéNav**, not vanilla guides.

**What does a biome category mean?** Spawn lines use categories like "savannas" or "forests." In this pack each category pulls in **many modded biomes**, not just the vanilla one — see the **[Biome Tag Reference](#biome-tag-reference)** at the bottom for the full list per category.
